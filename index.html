Enemies at the gates!

Your nation faces a dire threat; enemy forces come from the West while your forces are busy in the North. It falls to you, a lone commander on the front, to defend your homeland. Use the four Soul Crystals issued to you to hold off the enemy, but beware; should you overextend yourself defending from any particular wave, you will have insufficient resources for the next. Shepherd your resources carefully.
